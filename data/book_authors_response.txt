Pride and Prejudice: Jane Austen penned 'Pride and Prejudice', a novel primarily categorized as romance, and it was first published in 1813.
1984: George Orwell authored the dystopian novel ‘1984’, which falls under the genre of dystopian fiction and was first published in 1949.
The Hobbit: J.R.R. Tolkien wrote “The Hobbit,” which is primarily a fantasy adventure novel, and it was first published in 1937.
